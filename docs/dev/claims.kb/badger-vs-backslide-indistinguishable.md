In prior LLM physics discussions, the user could not reliably tell
whether persistence on a point was legitimate anti-backslide pressure or
badgering the LLM into accepting a claim. Grounds
[settlement-audit-trail]'s reopening clause, which makes the distinction
auditable after the fact by requiring something new at every reopening.
