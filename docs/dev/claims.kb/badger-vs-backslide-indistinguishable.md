In prior LLM physics discussions, the user could not reliably tell
whether persistence on a point was legitimate anti-backslide pressure or
badgering the LLM into accepting a claim. Grounds `R.SETTLE-AUDIT`'s
reopening clause, which makes the distinction auditable after the fact
by requiring something new at every reopening.
