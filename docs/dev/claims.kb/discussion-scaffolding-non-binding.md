Discussion-scope tooling (e.g. the discourse-graph pattern used here, or
adversarial review) is permitted precisely because it's declared
non-binding on the eventual G.SYS design. Proposed by the assistant as
the corollary to `C.DESIGN-FROM-SETTLED`; the user's subsequent scope
corrections (`C.DG-DISCUSS-ONLY`, `C.ADV-DISCUSS-ONLY`) are consistent
with it but it has not been explicitly ratified.
