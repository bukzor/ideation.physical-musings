A musing precisely refuted -- pinned to the exact theorem or observation
that kills it, with the understanding gained recorded -- is a success
outcome equal in standing to survival. Proposed by the assistant, not
yet ratified. Intended to defang adversarial review emotionally: the
skeptic opposes a musing's vagueness, not the musing itself.
