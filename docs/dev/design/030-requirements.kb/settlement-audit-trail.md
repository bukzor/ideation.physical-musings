Settled status must carry inspectable grounds, so evidence-settled is
distinguishable from fatigue-settled. Derived from `C.BADGER-LINE`: the
user can't always tell whether persistence on a point is legitimate
challenge or badgering. Resolution: reopening a settled node is always
permitted, but must present something new -- evidence, a flaw in prior
reasoning, or a clarified misunderstanding -- and that something-new
gets recorded at reopening, making the badger/backslide line auditable
after the fact.
