G.SYS is *healthy*, never *achieved* -- an invariant maintained under
growth, not a milestone. At every point: each settled claim carries
inspectable grounds ([settlement-audit-trail]); new sessions neither
silently contradict nor re-litigate it; reopening is always permitted
but must present something new. Metric: regressions + re-litigations per
session, held near zero as the corpus grows -- green CI, not a shipped
release. The rate itself is a live G.CONTIMP metric once instrumented
([should-we-mine-transcripts-for-a-baseline] covers establishing a
before/after baseline from prior transcripts).
